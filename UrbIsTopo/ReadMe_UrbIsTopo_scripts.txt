WARNING

DO NOT use this script to bulk import into OSM.

Data quality can be very fluctuating depending of : The zone, age of the poi (there is many items outdatted), etc ... Always do a aerial AND a streetview check before any editing.

Size of GeoJson generated can be large too , please take this in consideration depending on your setup.

Bench and trees are the most correctly georeferenced thinks in topo dataset , reason i haven't generated candidate for other topo points.
